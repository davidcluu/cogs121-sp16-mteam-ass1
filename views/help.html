About iWitness News

iWitness News aims to give users the inside scoop on events happening right here, right now. Made for the Snapchat and Instagram generation, iWitness News allows users to get first-hand accounts of all the crazy and wild things happening in our world today. Users can browse through videos related to a user-generated Headline, post their own video response, or create a new Headline all of their own!

New to iWitness News?

  1. Login with Twitter.
  2. Click a Headline to view related Video Responses that other users have posted!
  3. Contribute your own Headlines and Video Responses!
